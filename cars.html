THE EVOLUTION OF CARS SINCE ITS FIRST CREATEDS

The early history of the automobile can be divided into a number of eras, based on the prevalent means of propulsion. An automobile, autocar, motor car or car is a wheeled motor vehicle used for transporting passengers, which also carries its own engine or motor. Most definitions of the term specify that automobiles are designed to run primarily on roads, to have seating for one to eight people, to typically have four wheels, and to be constructed principally for the transport of people rather than goods.
